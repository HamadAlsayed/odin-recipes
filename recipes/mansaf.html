Classic Mansaf

Mansaf is definitely a unique dish in so many ways, from its ingredients to the way it tastes. A very traditional Jordanian dish that is served at any big gathering, from weddings to funerals.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lamb meat
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 crystals gum Arabic
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 13 Allspice berries
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 2 cups rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Pinch of saffron
  • 1 laban jameed
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 loaves shrak or tortillas
  • 4 tablespoons almonds
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
